About

Designed in Melbourne, Australia.

Roamboard exists for the tiny travel problem that becomes annoying every single night: too many Australian devices and one awkward overseas wall socket.

Why we made it

Travel adapters solve one plug. Roamboard is designed for the whole setup: phone, laptop, camera battery, headphones and the extra charger that always appears at the bottom of your bag.

Designed for real rooms

Hotels, Airbnbs and share houses are not designed around your charging routine. Roamboard gives your travel tech a cleaner anchor without making your luggage feel like a hardware drawer.

Local by nature

The brand, product direction and customer support are based in Melbourne, built around the way Australians actually travel overseas.
