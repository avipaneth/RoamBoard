Compatibility

Built for popular Type C, E and F destinations.

Roamboard is made for Australian plug devices when travelling to countries that use compatible European-style wall sockets.

Works in many popular destinations

Common compatible destinations include France, Germany, Spain, Portugal, Greece, Austria, Netherlands, Belgium, Croatia, Turkey, Indonesia including Bali, South Korea and Morocco.

Not a voltage converter

Roamboard does not convert voltage. Check that your device supports the voltage and frequency of your destination before plugging it in.

Not direct-fit everywhere

The UK, USA, Japan and Singapore use different wall socket types, so Roamboard is not intended to plug directly into those sockets without the right additional adapter.
