FAQ

Useful answers before you pack.

Is Roamboard a travel adapter or a powerboard?

Roamboard is a compact travel powerboard designed for Australians using AU plug devices overseas. It gives you multiple Australian sockets and USB-C ports from one travel setup.

Can I use Roamboard in Europe?

Roamboard is intended for Type E/F style destinations. In Europe, that commonly includes Albania, Andorra, Austria, Belarus, Belgium, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Bulgaria, Croatia, Czech Republic, Estonia, Finland, France, Georgia, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Iceland, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Moldova, Monaco, Montenegro, Netherlands, North Macedonia, Norway, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Serbia, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden, Turkey and Ukraine.

It may also suit other Type E/F destinations such as Indonesia, including Bali, South Korea, Morocco and Egypt. Always check the socket type, voltage and your device compatibility before travelling.

Does Roamboard convert voltage?

No. Roamboard is not a voltage converter. Always check that your device supports the voltage and frequency of the country you are travelling to before plugging it in.
